About

Hello, my name is Ludovic Lorant, I am a UI/UX designer based in Belgium. I started my career as a jack-of-all-trades webmaster, dealing with web design, front-end and back-end development. Along the way, I gradually started to specialize only in design work with UX in mind.

I worked in various fields such as media post-production (Stay Tuned), online music licensing (Jamendo) and digital assessment (O.A.T.).

I strive to build "user first", accessible, expandable, modular and goal-oriented designs. I particularly like the creation of design systems. I have a love for minimalism and efficiency before everything else.
