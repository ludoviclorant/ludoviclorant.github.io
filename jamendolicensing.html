Jamendo Licensing

About Jamendo

Jamendo is a music website created in 2005 which provides free music download and streaming for Internet users, while allowing artists to sell commercial licenses of their music for commercial use, such as music synchronization for audiovisual creations or background music in a public space through Licensing.

Roles

  • UI Design
  • Front-end (HTML/CSS)
  • Art Direction
  • Prototyping
  • Branding & Identity
  • Print & Social media
  • Copywriting

Upon my arrival at Jamendo in 2013 it quickly became clear that the streaming and licensing services, provided under the same hood, were conflicting with each other. The idea of separating the free streaming aspect of Jamendo and the professionnal licensing product stack became a new vision for the company online presence. This is how the idea of Jamendo Licensing was born.

I then worked on an MVP for a new website. The main goal was to provide an overall better design and user experience accross the board. Particular attention was put on providing a better communication on the multiple products (in-store, catalog and composition) and the creation of SEO specific landing pages including automated ones. This project included also a complete redesign of the client and reseller spaces.

A first version was realeased in December 2014. A new visual identity for the brand was created in 2015 alongside the develompment of Jamendo Music and was implemented in the website design iteratively during the years that followed.

Also, as the sole designer of the company I've been continuously delivering print, social media and email designs during my time there and was also heavily involved in copywriting.
