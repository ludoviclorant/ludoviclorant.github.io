About Jamendo

Jamendo is a music website created in 2005 which provides free music download and streaming for Internet users, while allowing artists to sell commercial licenses of their music for commercial use, such as music synchronization for audiovisual creations or background music in a public space through Licensing.

Roles

  • UI Design
  • Front-end (HTML/CSS)
  • Art Direction
  • Prototyping
  • Branding & Identity
  • Print & Social media
  • Copywriting

Upon my arrival at Jamendo in 2013 it quickly became clear that the streaming and licensing services, provided under the same hood, were conflicting with each other. The idea of separating the free streaming aspect of Jamendo and the professionnal licensing product stack became a new vision for the company online presence. This is how the idea of Jamendo Music was born.

I created a new visual identity for the new brand then worked on a MVP for a new website. During this time a starter design system was build along the design and integration phase.

The main goal was to create a fully new UI implementing the new identity and also make it a fully responsive cross-platform product. Particular attention was put on redesigning the player, providing better functionality for the community of listeners (search, suggestions, sharing, comment,..) and to put the artists and curated content under the spotlight for more engagement.

Released in its first version in October 2015, Jamendo Music then evolved iteratively during the years that followed.

Also, as the sole designer of the company I've been continuously delivering print, social media and email designs during my time there and was also heavily involved in copywriting.
